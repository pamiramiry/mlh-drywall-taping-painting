Popcorn Ceiling Removal · Scarborough, ON

Popcorn Ceiling Removal in Scarborough

A dated popcorn ceiling you've been putting off for years is a same-week job for our crew, scraped, skim-coated, and repainted for a modern, updated look.

Get a Free Quote Call (647) 531-8731

We scrape the existing texture, skim-coat the ceiling smooth or apply a modern re-texture, then prime and repaint. Most residential rooms are done within the same week. Dust containment and floor protection are standard on every job, so the rest of your home stays clean while we work.

Popcorn ceilings in homes built before the early 1980s can contain asbestos in the texture material. We recommend testing before removal if your home falls in that range. We're happy to advise on next steps and can pause the quote process until results are back.

Every popcorn ceiling removal quote is free, and since we handle the taping, mudding, and painting in-house, the finished ceiling gets the same multi-coat standard as a brand-new install.
