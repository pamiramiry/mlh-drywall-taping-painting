Tiling · Scarborough, ON

Tile Installation Contractor in Scarborough

Backsplash, shower, bathroom, and floor tile installation for kitchens, bathrooms, and commercial spaces across Scarborough and the GTA, with precise layout, a level substrate, and clean grout lines.

Get a Free Quote Call (647) 531-8731

From a kitchen backsplash to a full marble shower surround, our tile work starts with a level, properly prepped substrate and a careful dry layout before a single tile is set. That's what keeps grout lines straight and pattern joints consistent across a whole wall or floor.

Since we already handle the drywall and paint on most projects, tiling is a natural extension of the same job: one crew from framing through to the finished tile, with no gap between trades and no scheduling around a separate tile contractor.

We tile both residential kitchens and bathrooms and commercial spaces. From a backsplash refresh to a full commercial washroom, our tile work gets the same attention to layout and finish regardless of scale.
